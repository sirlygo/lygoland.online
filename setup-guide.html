System Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • OS: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14+, or modern Linux
  • CPU: Intel Core i3 or AMD equivalent (2.5 GHz+)
  • RAM: 4 GB (8 GB recommended)
  • GPU: Intel HD Graphics 4000 or dedicated GPU
  • Storage: At least 2 GB free space
  • Internet: Broadband connection

Recommended for Best Experience:

  • CPU: Intel Core i5/Ryzen 5 or better
  • RAM: 8 GB or more
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1050 / AMD RX 560 or better
  • Storage: SSD for faster load times

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I play on Bedrock Edition (Xbox, Mobile, PS5)?

Yes, You can connect to Lygoland.online with either java or bedrock.

Q: Is Minecraft free?

No, Minecraft Java Edition costs a one-time purchase. There are no subscription fees to play once purchased.

Q: What version should I use?

We recommend Minecraft 1.20.1.

Q: Do I need mods to play on Lygoland?

No mods are required! Our server uses plugins (server-side only) that work with vanilla Minecraft.

Q: I'm getting "Failed to connect" error. Help?

Common fixes: 1) Check your internet connection, 2) Verify the server address is exactly lygoland.online, 3) Restart Minecraft, 4) Check if the server is online at our status page.

Q: Can I use cracked Minecraft?

Yes, our server do not require legitimate Minecraft accounts . Please concider purchasing the game to support the developers.

Q: My game is laggy. How can I improve performance?

Try these: 1) Lower video settings (render distance, particles, graphics: fast), 2) Allocate more RAM to Minecraft in the launcher (Edit Profile ‚Üí More Options ‚Üí JVM Arguments: change -Xmx2G to -Xmx4G if you have 8GB+ RAM), 3) Close other programs while playing.

Q: Where can I get help if stuck?

Join our Discord server! Our friendly community and staff are always ready to help with setup issues, gameplay questions, or anything else.
